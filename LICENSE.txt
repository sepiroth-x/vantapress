TECHNOLOGY STACK:

VantaPress is built with:
- Laravel 11.47.0 (PHP Framework)
- FilamentPHP 3.3.45 (Admin Panel)
- PHP 8.2+ (Programming Language)
- MySQL/MariaDB (Database)
- Eloquent ORM (Data Layer)

No Node.js, npm, Vite, or build tools required for deployment!
